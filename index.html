This session is for parents of children aged 5–8 who feel stuck in a cycle of shouting, guilt, and disconnection. It is especially for parents of sensitive, intense, or differently-wired children who have tried the usual advice and found it doesn't hold.

If you often feel overstimulated, carry invisible mental load, and wonder whether something is wrong with your child or with your parenting — this is for you.

We start with the friction point — the exact moment that keeps breaking you. You tell me what it is, and I listen without judgement.

Then we name the wish: what you want to happen instead. Then we look at the fear underneath it all.

From there, I give you a personalised method to reconnect with your child in that specific moment — and a way to interrupt the yelling cycle before it takes over. Most parents feel the shift during the session itself.

Just come with one real situation — the moment that keeps repeating. You do not need notes, a plan, or anything prepared in advance.

If you included your friction point in the booking form, I will have read it before we meet and we can get straight into the work.

Therapy often looks at your past. This looks at the moment in front of you right now.

We are not diagnosing your child, working through your childhood, or building a long-term treatment plan. We are identifying the specific situation that is breaking you and giving you something practical to use immediately.

This is not therapy. It is a reset — targeted, direct, and focused on relief you can feel today.

A diagnosis adds context — it does not change the approach. I work with parents of children with sensory processing differences, ADHD, autism spectrum profiles, anxiety, and learning differences.

Understanding how your child's nervous system works is exactly the kind of insight this session provides. This work starts from understanding, not from the label.
