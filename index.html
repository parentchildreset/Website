About Your Coach

I’ve seen this from both sides of the table.

After 7 years working directly with children in classrooms — including kids with learning differences, behavioural challenges, and complex home situations — I started to see the same patterns playing out in family after family.

Parents who were trying everything. Children who couldn’t explain why they were struggling. And a gap between them that nobody knew how to close.

I now work directly with parents to bridge that gap. Not with more strategies or behaviour charts — but by helping you understand what’s actually driving the behaviour, so you can respond in a way that actually works.
